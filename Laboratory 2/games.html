Call of Duty Mobile

"Call of Duty: Mobile" is a free-to-play FPS for iOS and Android, blending classic Call of Duty multiplayer modes like Team Deathmatch and Domination with a battle royale mode. Players engage in fast-paced combat, customize loadouts, and progress through a battle pass. Featuring maps, weapons, and characters from the Call of Duty universe, the game regularly updates with new content and events to keep gameplay dynamic and engaging.
